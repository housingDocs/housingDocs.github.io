Themes
Change the background around your plot using Themes
Introduction to Themes
Themes are premade landscapes, structures and backgrounds surrounding the main Housing plot.
They can add a certain mood or style to a build without the player having to do too much.
Themes come in 5 tiers ⏤ Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary and Restricted ⏤ with themes in each tier costing more to obtain.
There are also a few seasonal themes tied to Holidays, Summer, or Halloween.
When creating a new house, the Default theme is automatically selected.

Themes menu can be accessed through:
Housing Menu >> Themes (Marked with Painting)

Toggle Carpenter
The themes page also contains Toggle Carpenter as an option, allowing you to toggle [NPC] Carpenter being spawned in your house.
List of Themes
* Costs are in Mystery Dust.
Mystery dust is used to purchase most themes, while seasonal themes have to be purchased through the event shop while an event is running.
As of August 2025, there are 84 themes in Housing.
