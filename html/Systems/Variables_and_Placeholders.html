Variables and Placeholders
Display stats with variables and placeholders
Introduction to Variables and Placeholders
Variables are stats of a player (player stat), a team (team stat) or the house (global stat).
These three types of stats will be explained in detail later in this article.

Placeholders are strings of text to use in order to display a statistic. These include, but are not limited to:
House stats (e.g. numbers of cookies or guests), time, and player info.
Stats/Variables
Variables may be incremented/decremented/multiplied/divided/set/unset* using the action "Change Variable".
* Unsetting a stat removes it from the player. In older versions, that was done by setting the stat to 0.
Within said action, the developer may switch the type of stat to change with the "Holder" option:
Player, Global, or Team.

> Player stats are separated between every player that has joined the house.
These stats will be wiped after a duration set by the house owner (14d max, 365d with Housing+)
Example: Player health
Placeholder: %var.player/(key)% or %stat.player/(key)%
> Team stats are separated for each team within the house instead of each player.
Example: Team wins/losses
Placeholder: %var.team/(key)% or %stat.team/(key)%
> Global stats behave similarly to team stats, except they are shared throughout the whole house.
Example: Countdown timer
Placeholder: %var.global/(key)% or %stat.global/(key)%
/variable Command
"/var" may also be used.
/variable [type] [action] [player] [key] (amount)
[type] >> player/global/team, referring to the type of stat
[action]* >> inc/dec/multi/div/set/unset/clear/list, referring to what is being done with /var
[player] >> name of a player
[key] >> target stat key (e.g. kills)
(amount) >> only used when [action] is one of the bolded actions

* Only basic actions are listed. For more variations or other actions, enter /var [type] in chat (and a space), then press tab.
Placeholders
Placeholders can be used in texts such as chat messages sent by actions or an item's lore.
In these texts, the placeholder used is replaced with its corresponding stat.
Please note that placeholders cannot be sent manually into chat. It does not work as a placeholder, and Hypixel blocks the message for "advertising".
List of placeholders: (variables excluded)
Placeholders marked with "~" are commonly used.
