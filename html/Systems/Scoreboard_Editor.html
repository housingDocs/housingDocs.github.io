Introduction to Scoreboard Editor
In every Housing, a scoreboard is shown on the right side of your screen.
Often it displays the Housing's name, the amount of guests, and the amount of cookies recieved.
However, you can redesign it and customize it to your own liking, including: adding or removing information.
Be aware! Due to limitations, the Scoreboard can only hold up to 10 items!
How to edit my scoreboard?
To edit your scoreboard, head into your Housing, and type in "/scoreboard".

To add a scoreboard item, click on Add Scoreboard Item (Marked as a paper)
To remove a scoreboard item, right click on the selected scoreboard item.
To change the order of the scoreboard items, press and hold shift, while left / right clicking any scoreboard item.
