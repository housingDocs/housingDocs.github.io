Teams
Organize players and enhance gameplay with Teams
Introduction to Teams
Teams are separate from groups, allowing houses to have a better way to display what team a player is on or what stage they may be in without the need to use permission-based groups.
You can create competitive environments, organize players for minigames, or simply display progression stages with customizable teams.

Please note that Teams are sorted in a random order!
Key Features:
Team Identity: Assign players to teams with custom names, colored tags, and unique stats.
Friendly Fire: Control whether teammates can damage each other when PVP is enabled.
Team Stats: Track and display custom statistics for each team, similar to player stats.
Global Settings: Configure how teams appear in chat, the player list, and how players are balanced across teams.
