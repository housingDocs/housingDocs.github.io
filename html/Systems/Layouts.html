Introduction to Inventory Layouts

Inventory Layouts allow for the swapping of a player's entire inventory at an instant when activated. Layouts can be applied through calling them from an action.

You can create Inventory Layouts by:

Entering Housing Menu > Systems (Activator Rail) > Inventory Layouts (Iron Axe) and creating a new layout by clicking the paper

Rows 1 to 3 represents the inventory of the player, while Row 5 represents the hotbar.
You may put any item into the layout and click Save Layout to make changes.
You may also use your own inventory as the layout by clicking Import Layout.

To replace the 9th slot of the hotbar in the layout, you must use a Give Item function to replace the 9th slot with a specific item, then import your inventory into the layout.
To know more about the Give Item function, check out this page.
