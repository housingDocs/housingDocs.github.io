Regions
Trigger an action when entering/exiting an area using Regions
Introduction to Regions
When a player enters or leaves a "Region", a customizable effect is triggered.
The Regions menu can be accessed through:
"/region" OR "/regions"
Housing Menu (Nether Star/Ghast Tear) > "Systems" (Activator Rail) > "Regions" (Grass Block)
How do I use Regions?
Run "//", "//wand" OR "/wand" to get the "Region Selection Tool".
Use the tool to select position A (left click a block) and position B (right click a block).
Open the Regions menu and click on "Create a Region" and name the Region.
Congratulations! You've created a Region. You can now customize its actions when a player enters/exit the Region.
Editing a Region
"Rename Region" (Name Tag) – Set the Region’s name after the first time you named it
"Teleport Region" (Ender Pearl) – Teleport to the northwestern corner of the Region
"Move Region" (Stick) – Move the Region to your current Region Selection Tool area
"PvP Settings" (Iron Sword) – Toggle damage and PvP settings (e.g. Fall Damage, Keep Inventory)
"Entry Actions" (Paper) – Set actions triggered when a player enters the Region
"Exit Actions" (Paper) – Set actions triggered when a player leaves the Region
"Delete Region" (TNT) – Delete the Region after confirmation
