Jukebox (Media Player)
Add music and vibe to your Housing using Jukebox
Introduction to Jukebox

Jukebox is a VIP (or higher) feature that allows you to play non-Minecraft music within your Housing plot.
You can set the atmosphere, create themed areas, or host events with personalized soundtracks provided by Hypixel.

Key Features:

Play Music: Select songs to play in order for all players in your Housing plot.
Atmosphere Control: Choose to set the perfect mood for your build.
Global Audio: The music is heard by everyone on the plot (unless they used /togglemusic
How to activate Jukebox:
Simply enable Jukebox from the Housing Menu, choose from a variety of music by selecting any out of 37 songs, and enjoy the tunes.
You may also enable or disable all songs at once.
