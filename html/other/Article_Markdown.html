Text Blocks
Just normal text
Beneath a sky painted in fading gold, whispers of forgotten stories danced through the tall grass, weaving memories into the breeze. Shadows stretched like fingers across the earth, and somewhere in the distance, a bell rang for no one, echoing through the stillness like a question never answered.
An ordered List
Drinking enough water boosts your energy and concentration.
Regular exercise improves mood and reduces stress.
Reading daily can expand your knowledge and vocabulary.
Getting enough sleep helps your body recover and stay healthy.
A HTSL code block
if and (stat kills > 10) {
chat "Kills > 10"
} else {
chat "Kills aren't > 10"
}
The Blocks in Markdown:
<text>Beneath a sky painted in fading gold, whispers of forgotten stories danced through the tall grass, weaving memories into the breeze. Shadows stretched like fingers across the earth, and somewhere in the distance, a bell rang for no one, echoing through the stillness like a question never answered.</text>

<list>
<point>Drinking enough water boosts your energy and concentration.</point>
<point>Regular exercise improves mood and reduces stress.</point>
<point>Reading daily can expand your knowledge and vocabulary.</point>
<point>Getting enough sleep helps your body recover and stay healthy.</point>
</list>

<code>if and (stat kills > 10) {
chat "Kills > 10"
} else {
chat "Kills aren't > 10"
}</code>
Other Elements
An external Link (in this case in a header)
<header><link ref="https://google.com">A link</link> (in this case inside of a header)</header>
Linking to another Article
When linking to another article link only the "location" (eg. the location of https://cookieCrood.github.io/html/home/Introduction.html is "/html/home/Introduction.html")
<header>Linking to another <link ref"/html/home/Introduction.html">Article</link></header>
