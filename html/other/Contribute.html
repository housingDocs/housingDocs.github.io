Contribute
How to contribute, what to contribute and benefits of contributions
How to contribute?
Everyone can submit an article for the Documentation in the Discord. However, articles must pass verification in order to be added.

The verification requirements are:
The article is valuable to the Documentation (nothing offtopic)
The article is professional (good grammar, etc.)
The article covers something new (no duplicates, exceptions for Priorities)
What to contribute?
You can contribute anything that is related to Housing. With the exception of:
Articles about specific Players
Articles about specific Houses
Articles about specific Events
Articles that cover Priorities are more likely to be verified
Priorities
Prioritized article topics can be found in the Discord. These are topics that need to be covered as soon as possible
