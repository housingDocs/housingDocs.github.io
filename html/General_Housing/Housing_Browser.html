Introduction to Housing Browser

The Housing Browser is a menu used to explore and visit other players' houses. It is accessible through the Browse Houses NPC in the Housing Lobby or through the Housing Menu and clicking on the spruce door.

There are five rows of houses, each of which can be scrolled through to see more houses. There is also a paper at the bottom of the menu which allows the player to input another person's name to see and visit their houses.

Channels

Each row of houses is sorted based on a specific category or "channel." By default, the selected channels will be Most Guests, Weekly cookies, MVP++, Recommended, and Social.
Clicking on the icons (left side of menu) will open the channel selection screen, where the channel of the row selected can be changed. To reset the channels list, click on the TNT icon.
There are currently 45 channels including regular, tag and language channels.
