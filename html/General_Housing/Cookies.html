Introduction to Cookies
Cookies are a rating system used specifically for housing. Houses with more cookies will tend to get increased traffic and players joining, because it would show up in the Most Cookies leaderboard in the Housing Browser.

When joining a house, players will get cookies in the middle (5th) slot of their hotbar unless it is overridden by an inventory layout.Right clicking on this item will give the creator a pack of cookies which varies based on the rank the player has.

Each week, players are given 10 packs of cookies to give to creators.
Cookie counts for every house reset on each Sunday at 12AM (Toronto Time) to allow more dynamic changes on the leaderboard and cycle top houses.
