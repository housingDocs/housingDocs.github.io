Navigating the Documentation
Using the Navigation Bar
The navigation bar on the left side of the Page offers a variety of Articles. Additionally, the articles are also grouped by their general topic. For example: an article about Event Actions would be grouped in Systems.
Visiting exact address (Not recommended)
Optionally you can type out the exact address of an article, which is neither time-efficient nor actually beneficial. Example: Visiting https://cookiecrood.github.io/html/home/Introduction.html directly.
